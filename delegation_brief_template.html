📝 Task Overview

Start by getting clear on the basics. Who is doing what, and when does it need to be done?

Context helps people prioritise properly

✅ What “Done” Looks Like

Describe the finished result in concrete terms. What will you see, read, or receive when this is complete? Be specific — vague definitions create vague results.

Be clear about scope so nothing unexpected lands back on your desk

⭐ The Standard Required

This is where most delegation breaks down. Be honest about what level of quality this task actually needs.

Tone, format, brand guidelines, anything that matters to you
What have you had to fix before? Say it upfront.

📖 Resources & Context

Give them everything they need before they start — not after they get stuck.

📅 Check-In Points

Agree check-ins upfront. This removes the need to hover — and gives them confidence to move forward without you.
