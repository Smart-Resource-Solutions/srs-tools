🔍 Why This Role Exists

Before anything else — be honest about why you’re creating this role. Reactive hires usually start with no clear answer to this question.

What specific problem does it solve? Be honest — “I’m overwhelmed” is a valid answer.

🏆 What Success Looks Like in 90 Days
